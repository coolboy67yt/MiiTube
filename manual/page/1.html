About MiiTube

MiiTube is a platform (created by Jacane_U and others)
that collects all the videos and YouTube channels related
to Miis, the Wii, Wii U, 3DS, and more.

Using MiiTube

Using MiiTube is very simple.
You can navigate through the different sections using
the navigation bar at the top of the page!

- Videos Section:
In the "Videos" section, you can browse and watch videos.

- MiiTubers Section:
In the "MiiTubers" section, you can find a list of all the MiiTubers on the platform and their channels.
You can click on a MiiTuber's name to view their profile and see all their videos.

- Manual Section:
The "Manual" section provides helpful articles and guides on how to use MiiTube.

2: I want to become a MiiTuber!

Do you have a YouTube channel and think it could become a MiiTube channel? Then follow these steps!

Check if your channel meets the following criterias:

  1. Your channel must have published at least one LONG video.
  2. Almost all/all videos on your channel must be related to Miis, Wii, WiiU, 3DS, etc...
  3. It is recommended to have a Mii as your profile picture, however this is only optional.
  4. Your channel must have quality content. For what defines 'quality content', please contact us.
  5. Your channel must not violate YouTube's community guidelines.
  6. Your channel must not be a reupload channel (channels that reupload other people's content without permission)
  7. Your channel must not be a spam channel (channels that post repetitive or irrelevant content)
  8. The videos you upload to your channel must have been created with effort (e.g., not low quality AI-generated content, or content you created in a short time)
  9. No brainrot content.

My channel is ready, how do I become a MiiTuber now?

At the moment, we are still working on our backend. For now, you will
have to contact us with your channel information.

What information do I need to give?

  • Your channel's profile picture
  • Your YouTube channel name
  • A brief description of your channel
  • Your channel's link address
We will then reply to you letting you know if your channel has been accepted.
